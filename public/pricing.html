Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Oregon Legal Mind.ai different?

We're the first legal AI platform with a voice assistant (LegalBot) that prospects can call to experience our technology. Plus, our local deployment options ensure complete attorney-client privilege protection.

How does LegalBot work?

LegalBot is our AI voice assistant that handles phone calls 24/7. Prospects can call to learn about our platform while actually experiencing our AI capabilities during the conversation.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We offer cloud hosting with enterprise-grade security, plus on-premises deployment options for complete data control. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

Can I integrate with my existing tools?

Yes! We integrate with popular legal practice management software, document management systems, and billing platforms. Custom integrations are available for Enterprise clients.

What kind of support do you provide?

Support levels vary by plan: email support for Starter, priority phone/email for Small Firm, dedicated account manager for Mid-Firm, and 24/7 white-glove support for Enterprise.

Do you offer training?

Yes! All plans include basic onboarding. Mid-Firm and Enterprise tiers include comprehensive training programs and ongoing education for your team.
